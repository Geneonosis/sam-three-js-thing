The path to mainstream 3D was bumpy but steady.

  1. 2011: WebGL lands everywhere, giving direct GPU access from JavaScript.
  • 2013+: Three.js abstracts the rough edges and grows a massive ecosystem.
  • Today: WebGPU promises modern graphics APIs, better tooling, and higher performance.
